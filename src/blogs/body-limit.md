By default, the [web](/blog/learn-web-dev-better/) is responsive. Your CSS can make it better or [worse](/blog/dangerous-css/). One of the ways to make the web's responsiveness worse is to set the `width` and `height` of the HTML `body` element. You must not do that. Let the page adjust automatically.
