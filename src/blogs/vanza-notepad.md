[Vanza Notepad](https://notepad.vanzasetia.xyz/) is a free/libre and open source web application. It is also a simple progressive web app. You can use it for a disposable note-taking app. This means you use it for taking quick, temporary notes for whatever you are doing without having to delete anything manually.

But you can use it however you like.

I built this web app with full awareness of my users' environment. You use a browser. This means I do not need to develop something the browser has provided.

You can take notes quickly. Unlike text editors or other heavy web applications, Vanza Notepad loads quickly even on a slow internet connection. Once it is loaded in your browser, you can install it, and it can fully function without any internet connection.

Vanza Notepad works with your browser. Since you are in a browser, you can search while taking notes without having to open another app.

If you change your note in another tab, Vanza Notepad automatically syncs it up with all tabs.

Psychologically speaking, you can use Vanza Notepad for a safety mode. I am not a psychologist. But I like to say to you that when you are nervous, it can be useful for you. During panic moments, you can use this web app for writing without accidentally sending messages. Write down your text in Vanza Notepad first. Once you are sure, copy your important messages and paste them in your chat application.

Moreover, you can delete notes quickly too. Your browser allows you to delete data easily. You do not need to go to your operating system's settings.

Additionally, you do not want to risk your privacy and security by installing a new app.

Of course, I can keep telling you the good things, and here are more benefits and values of Vanza Notepad:

- [Lightweight](/blog/lightweight-website/)
- Distraction-free
- [Privacy-focused](/blog/website-does-not-track-you/)
- No AI
- Humans first
- A light theme and a dark theme
- Ad-free
- No tracking
- Works offline (as PWA)
- No registration required
- Auto-saving (as you type)
- Beginner-friendly project (using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript)

Please [use Vanza Notepad](https://notepad.vanzasetia.xyz/) and share it. Hack it if you want and [make it yours](https://github.com/vanzasetia/vanza-notepad).

Happy noting!
