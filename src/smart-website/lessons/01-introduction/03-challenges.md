title: Facing challenges
---

Before you begin, you should be aware of the challenges you may face when developing a personal website. The challenges are the following:

- you do not know what to do;
- nobody visits your website; and
- nobody reads your blog posts; and
- nobody gives you a response or a comment.

This course exists to help you face those challenges.
