Having a place on the internet where you can share your knowledge and opinions with everyone is nice. You do not have to be limited to 280 characters. Your post can be unique with different visual looks. You are free. You are in control. You can do whatever you want with your website. But with complete freedom, you may not know what to do. This guide helps you to know what to do and how to develop a personal website.

This course will not teach you how to write code in great detail. I only touch the surface of it. In fact, I assume that you already know how to write code.

This course focuses on *developing* a personal website. That means this course is meant for a long-term process. Unlike other courses that focus on building a website and leaving you with nothing, this course gives you hints as you develop your website to be better.

Information in this course is information that I rarely find available in tutorials. This means I may say something new, and I ask you to think about what I say.

This course gives philosophical ideas to prevent people from thinking that all websites need a fancy appearance and popular tools.

Enjoy this course. Try the new ideas. Put into practice the knowledge you get from here. Have fun!
