title: When people finally understand, they feel good
---

One of the ways to make your users feel good is to make them smarter. When was the last time you got confused and then finally understood it? I bet you felt good the moment you understood it.

We can make people feel good by having good writing. If we can make them a little bit happier with our writings, why don't we do that?

If you have a talent as a teacher, having a personal website is a good opportunity to showcase your teaching skills. You may be able to explain JavaScript in plain English. If you are good at joking, you can add jokes too. This way, more people will understand JavaScript, and at the same time, you make them feel better.
