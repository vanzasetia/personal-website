title: Your users' connection may matter
---

Not all users trust their Internet Service Providers (ISPs). Your users may use different ways to connect to the internet and to your website.

Disallowing a [Tor](https://www.torproject.org/) connection prevents your users from accessing your website with anonymity. Some internet users need to be anonymous, so they use a Tor connection to access the internet. Allow them to access your website through a Tor connection if possible.

VPN users also exist. Allowing VPN users is another way to increase your website's accessibility.

If your main users are people who use non-mainstream connections, allow them to access your website through different connections.
