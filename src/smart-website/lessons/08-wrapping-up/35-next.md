What do you feel? Do you want to discuss something with me? You can contact me through my email or make a post on social media, depending on your purpose.

I hope this course helps you develop your fantastic personal website.

I do not discuss Large Language Models (LLMs) or Artificial Intelligence (AI) here because those tools are just tools. Your circumstances and your attitude form your opinions on them. Those tools are useful, in my opinion. But they are not so useful for developing a personal website. I want you to develop a personal website and generate content from your brain. Those tools can be used to help you fix typos and grammar errors. But they should not tell you what to say. Feel free to use them however you want.
