I use Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Large Language Models (LLMs) to help me fix grammar and learn English. My native language is not English, by the way. I use AI to get suggestions when I need them.

I will not promote content that I suspect has been generated by AI.

I do not use generative AI to write an entire article and publish it here. For those who think otherwise, prove me wrong!
